## Ready After 499A Closeout

### 3. Choose The First CSE 499B Slice

**Decision needed:** Pick one path before implementation begins.

| Candidate | Why it is valuable | Keep out of the first slice |
|---|---|---|
| Verified route-specific context | Makes recommendations more trip-specific using source-backed facts such as water availability, remoteness, or cell-service risk. | Broad trail scraping or unverified logistics. |
| Live AI explanation provider | Exercises the existing guarded contract with a constrained provider call while preserving rule-engine authority. | AI-generated packing decisions or unbounded freeform input. |
| Curated trail expansion | Gives more hikers a complete, source-labeled experience. | Adding profiles without adequate official trail facts. |
| No-account save or export | Lets a hiker keep a finished list without account complexity. | Login, profiles, or persistent personal data. |

**Done when:** One candidate is selected, its user value and source/data limits are
written down, and it is broken into a small implementation plan.
